# SignalFire Executive Summary

## 1. Situation

Corporate strategy shifts are visible before they become explicit. Companies
signal future priorities through changes in disclosure language, management
commentary, restructuring activity, and industry narratives.

Consulting firms, investors, and corporate strategy teams need a systematic way
to detect these shifts early enough to shape account strategy, market entry
decisions, and transformation theses.

## 2. Complication

Traditional market intelligence is often reactive. By the time a company
announces an AI transformation, cloud migration, cybersecurity program, or cost
reduction initiative, competitors and advisors are already pursuing the same
opportunity.

The challenge is not collecting more text. The challenge is converting public
language into explainable strategic signals that answer:

- What is the company focusing on?
- What changed over time?
- Which priorities are accelerating?
- Which industries are transforming fastest?
- Where is advisory demand likely emerging?

## 3. Findings

SignalFire converts real public disclosures into a structured strategic
intelligence layer:

- **Strategic theme intensity** identifies which priorities dominate company
  language.
- **Theme momentum** detects whether a priority is rising or declining.
- **Priority growth rate** captures year-over-year acceleration.
- **Priority concentration** shows whether a company is focused or diffuse.
- **Consulting opportunity score** combines strategic change velocity, priority
  growth, industry pressure, and disruption signals.
- **Transformation readiness score** evaluates whether a company has consistent,
  broad, and stable strategic language supporting execution.

The platform supports executive views across companies, themes, and industries,
with auditable formulas and visible assumptions.

## 4. Strategic Implications

SignalFire can support several high-value use cases:

- **Consulting account prioritization:** identify companies where transformation
  demand is likely emerging.
- **Corporate strategy monitoring:** track competitors' language shifts by
  theme and industry.
- **Private equity diligence:** detect strategic modernization pressure across
  portfolio companies and targets.
- **Product strategy:** understand which enterprise priorities are accelerating
  by sector.
- **Market intelligence:** separate sustained transformation signals from
  episodic management commentary.

## 5. Recommendations

1. Use SignalFire as an early-warning layer for strategic account planning.
2. Review high-momentum company-theme pairs monthly and validate them against
   earnings calls, filings, and restructuring activity.
3. Prioritize companies with high consulting opportunity scores and moderate
   readiness scores, where demand is likely strong but execution support may be
   needed.
4. Extend the platform with hiring and skills data to improve transformation
   readiness and capability alignment.
5. Add source-level evidence packs so consultants can move from signal detection
   to client-ready briefing materials.
